      Surgery Department

      General Surgery

      It handles common surgeries such as appendectomy and cholecystectomy, using both conventional and laparoscopic surgery to ensure a faster recovery.

      Surgical Oncology

      Specializes in removing cancerous tumors, in cooperation with oncologists, using advanced techniques such as roboticsurgery.

      Colorectal Surgery

      Treats diseases of the rectum and colon such as cancer and infections using conventional surgery and endoscopy to reduce complications.

      Maxillofacial Surgery

      He deals with maxillofacial surgeries, such as fracture repair and bone grafting, using advanced digital techniques to improve function and appearance.

      Endocrine Surgery

      Treats endocrine disorders by removing the thyroid, parathyroid and adrenal glands, with modern techniques to ensure hormonal balance.

      Orthopedic Surgery

      He specializes in orthopedic and joint surgeries, such as fractures and joint replacement, using arthroscopy and robotics to improve movement and recovery.

      Spinal Surgery

      Treats diseases and injuries of the spine such as herniated disc and scoliosis using advanced techniques to relieve pain and improve function.

      Urologic Surgery

      Specializes in urinary and reproductive system surgeries such as prostatectomy and kidney stones, using endoscopy and robotic surgery.

      Pediatric Surgery

      Treats congenital anomalies and pediatric surgical emergencies using minimally invasive techniques to ensure a speedy recovery.

      Critical and Intensive Care

      Provides advanced care for critical conditions, such as trauma and organ failure, using the latest life support and medical monitoring technologies.

      Anesthesiology Department

      Supervises anesthesia during operations and pain management after surgery, using advanced techniques to ensure patient safety.

      Radiology Department

      Provides medical imaging services such as X-rays and MRI, to support diagnosis and guide operations and radiotherapy.
