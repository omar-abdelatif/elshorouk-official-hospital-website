      El Sherouk Hospital's Research Department aims to become a leading hub for medical innovation over the next five years. The plan includes establishing a state-of-the-art research center equipped with advanced laboratories and cutting-edge technology. Key priorities involve expanding clinical trials, fostering international collaborations, and developing groundbreaking treatments in surgery, regenerative medicine, and epidemiology. The department will also focus on training young researchers and integrating artificial intelligence into medical research. By 2030, El Sherouk Hospital envisions setting new standards in evidence-based healthcare and contributing significantly to global medical advancements

      El Sherouk Research Grant Sponsorship

      El Sherouk Hospitals actively supports medical research through its prestigious research grant sponsorship. This initiative provides funding to outstanding researchers who contribute significantly to medical advancements and innovation. The grant is awarded to individuals demonstrating excellence in scientific research and commitment to healthcare improvement. Winners are selected based on their research impact and potential for global contributions.
